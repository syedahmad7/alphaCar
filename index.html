About Us

Alpha Transportation was created by a group of friends in the Fall of 2023. The idea was generated when the group struggled to find a rental vehicle for their summer vacation. The group of friends rented a minivan from a national car rental company about a month before their vacation. Unfortunately, on the day of the trip, when the group arrived at the rental location, the rental company informed them that they were out of all minivans. The friends were frustrated to hear the news because there was no cancellation notice or any other correspondence from the rental company.

The group of friends was ready to start their vacation and could not let any obstacle come their way. They called and looked around a few other well-known car rental companies but the answer was the same for all. Fortunately, a rental option was available on Turo and it helped save the vacation.

During the vacation, the group decided that they would also start to host on Turo starting with a small fleet of vehicles and cater to the DFW and surrounding areas. The priority for the group will be to provide excellent customer service and stay on top of communication so that no one else has to go through the experience they had. That was the start of Alpha Transportation!

The Alpha Team takes pride in superior customer service. All vehicles under the Alpha Team are in excellent condition. If you have a rental need or have any questions, please reach out to us and a member of the Alpha Team will get back to you shortly.
